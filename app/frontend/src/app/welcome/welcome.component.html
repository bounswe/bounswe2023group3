Make predictions by participating in polls to earn points and badges

Welcome to Poll'up, a platform where users create and participate in prediction polls about various events and outcomes. Predictions are evaluated for their accuracy, contributing to users' prediction power scores. Events are categorized, and users' scores are tagged for a competitive experience. Users may control the visibility of collective predictions. Profile badges, achievements, and social features are designed to engage users.


Connect with Friends

Participate in Polls

Join prediction polls, make your own predictions, and compete with other users to earn points.

Earn badges and ranks

Complete achievements to earn different types of badges, and see your rank among all users.

Enrich your knowledge about events

Engaging in polls is not a necessity, you can also get information from already evaluated polls.
